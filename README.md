## 🖌 Overview  

SideNotes Professional Suite is a contextual notes environment designed around the insight that the best note-taking tool is one that appears instantly when needed and disappears just as quickly when you are done.

The panel design keeps notes at the periphery of your workflow. They are available the moment you need them and invisible when you do not, maintaining focus on the main work while keeping reference material always within reach.

Note organization through folders, colors, and tags lets you build a structured note library that serves both quick capture and longer-term reference storage within the same lightweight interface.

Drag-and-drop import from other applications and export to standard formats make SideNotes Professional Suite a capable component in larger workflows rather than an isolated scratchpad.

SideNotes Professional Suite is the notes companion for users who want their capture tool to add convenience without adding complexity, keeping the friction of note-taking low enough that they actually use it for everything.
